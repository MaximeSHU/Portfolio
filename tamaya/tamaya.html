About this project

Tamaya is an autonomous AI VTuber that lives inside Discord. She listens to voice conversations, decides on her own whether to respond, generates unpredictable replies through a local LLM, speaks with a neural voice, and animates a VTube Studio avatar — all in real time.

She has a real personality: bizarre, impulsive, sometimes mean, always strange. A free will system lets her spontaneously comment on conversations after a period of silence, making her feel genuinely alive in the server.

  • Voice recognition via Faster-Whisper (STT)
  • Local LLM with Mistral — no internet required
  • Neural voice synthesis with Edge TTS (fr-FR-EloiseNeural)
  • VTube Studio avatar animated via WebSocket (pyvts)
  • Free will — spontaneous comments after silence
  • Emotion mapping — hotkeys triggered by detected sentiment
  • Full Discord integration — !join, !leave, !reset commands

🧠
AI BRAIN
ai_brain.py
Drives Tamaya's entire personality. Interfaces with Ollama/Mistral locally. Manages conversation history, decides whether to respond, and can generate spontaneous comments unprompted — her "free will" system.
🎭
VTUBE MANAGER
vtube_manager.py
Connects to VTube Studio via WebSocket using pyvts. Maps emotions to hotkeys, triggers lip-sync animations in real time, and fires idle animations to keep the avatar alive between responses.
🗣️
SPEAK WORKER
speak_worker.py
Spawned as a subprocess. Generates speech audio via Edge TTS (fr-FR-EloiseNeural), writes it to a .wav file, and plays it either locally through VB-Audio Cable or directly into the Discord voice channel.
🤖
DISCORD BOT
main.py + discord_config.py
Handles the full Discord integration. Listens in voice channels, routes messages to the AI brain, plays audio responses back with FFmpeg, and exposes slash commands like !join, !leave, !status, !reset.
⚙️
CONFIG
config.py
Single source of truth. Controls the LLM model, personality prompt, Whisper model size, TTS settings, VTube port, free-will timing, and all runtime behavior — no hardcoded values anywhere else.
